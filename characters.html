Characters

Markus

Markus is a former member of the army. He deals with various forms of mental illness that stem from a traumatic childhood and his time in the military. Because of that childhood, Markus is very close to his little sister, Lily. He'd do anything to protect her. His parents were killed by a bear attack when he was young.

Lily

Lily is the little sister of Markus. Because of their close relationship, she plays a big role in his life and, thus, the story. She represents Markus's happiness. When her parents were killed, Lily sustained an injury that would forever hinder the use of her legs.

Vanessa

Damalum

Damalum, whose name means "Great Evil", is the leader of an army that has come from another universe to retrieve the artifacts that Markus and Lily possess.

Sorge

Sorge, whose name means "sadness", is the "dark" version of Markus. All of Markus's ghosts and closeted skeletons come out in Sorge.

Ketszembe

Ketszembe is a hard character to understand, that is why his name means "two-faced". He is representative of bipolar disorder, resulting in his ever-changing relationship with Markus and Lily.
